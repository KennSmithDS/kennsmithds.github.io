Choropleth Map - % of survey respondents who always wear a mask

The below choropleth map is based on a survey conducted by the New York Times that depicts the % of survey respondents for a given county that always wear a face mask. This is important because it shows which states and counties have either progressive populations, or maybe have stricter mandates. These added assumptions would need to be validated with more research into COVID mandates in those regions.
